Chapter V
Sound Wave · PK Battle
Two players each choose an instrument and fire wave attacks — higher frequency deals more damage. Waves cancel out if they share the same frequency and amplitude!
How to Play
① Choose Instrument
Each player picks a different instrument from the panel below. Each has unique harmonics and a damage bonus.
② Set Frequency
Drag the frequency slider — higher frequency = higher damage per attack. Range: 100 Hz to 2000 Hz.
③ Set Amplitude
Adjust the amplitude slider (1–10). Higher amplitude = larger wave projectile and more bonus damage.
④ Fire!
Press ⚡ Attack. A wave packet flies across the arena toward your opponent and deals damage on impact.
⑤ Wave Cancellation
If both players fire waves with the same frequency and amplitude simultaneously, the waves cancel each other out — no damage!
⑥ Win Condition
Reduce the opponent's HP to 0 first. Watch the HP bars — they glow red when health is critically low!
