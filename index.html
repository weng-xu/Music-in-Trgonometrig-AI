Chapter V
Sound Wave · PK Battle
Two players each choose an instrument and fire wave attacks — higher frequency deals more damage. Waves cancel out if they share the same frequency and amplitude!
How to Play
① Choose Instrument
Each player picks an instrument. Each has unique harmonics and a damage bonus.
② Set Frequency
Higher frequency = higher damage. Range: 100–2000 Hz.
③ Set Amplitude
Amplitude 1–10. Higher = larger wave and more bonus damage.
④ Fire!
Press ⚡ Attack. A wave packet flies across toward your opponent.
⑤ Wave Cancellation
Same frequency AND amplitude from both players → waves cancel — no damage!
⑥ Win Condition
Reduce the opponent's HP to 0 first.
